"We’re Indian Carriers, doing what we do best"

Connect with the world, connect with the journey. Delivering it right the first time, on time. Distance is just a detail, we take care of the rest. We are Indian Carriers, doing what we do best! Indian Carriers created on 7th July 2023. We can become your new partner for “Virtual Trucking Transportation” because of our wide experience across in-game maps. We will be glad to help you to improve your Virtual Trucking Experience. Founding members of Indian Carriers are experienced in the Virtual Trucking and we want to create well-known & well-settled VTC for our fellow Indians. Indian Carriers is an active community specialized in Euro Truck Simulator 2 & American Truck Simulator. We hope every Indian to join us and make your Virtual Trucking experience much more joyous and adventurous.
